Cajun Pain Perdu & Pancakes

Cajun Pain Perdu

Down in New Orleans you can get this wonderful French Toast called pain Perdu (which means lost bread). Maple trees won’t grow very well in the Delta, so we make our own syrup of honey rum and spices. We finish our pain perdu with roasted pecans and whipped cream.
