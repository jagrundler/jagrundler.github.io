Catering

Bon Temps offers a wide range of catering services for:

  • Weddings
  • Anniversaries
  • Corporate Meetings
  • Festivals
  • Retirement Parties
  • Mardi Gras Parties
  • Crawfish Boils
  • Barbecues

We can accommodate parties up to 400 people and can provide non-Creole menu items as well. Prices range from $15.00 to $28.00 per person. Please contact us to set up your perfect party or function.
