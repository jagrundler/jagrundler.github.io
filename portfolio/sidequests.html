Drawings’ Progressions

When I moved out for college, my mom told me she missed me doing art at the kitchen table and seeing the progress. I started making albums with hourly(ish) pictures of my work to share with her. These have been surprisingly helpful for me, as they remind me to worry less about how infinitely far away "done" feels and focus more on iteration & trusting my process.
