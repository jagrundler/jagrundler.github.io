Shelter in Place Website

Seven week website creation project

This project was assigned at the start of the pandemic. We were to create a “Shelter in Place” website that demonstrated what we were up to while staying home. Each week involved learning new skills via minor assignments, ultimately incorporating those features into our designs, and updating them on GitHub. The goal of the course was to familiarize ourselves with HTML, CSS, and JS; particularly, to hone & solidify knowledge from previous quarters.
