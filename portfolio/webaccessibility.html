Web Accessibility Project

designing & prototyping a browser extension for improved visual web accessibility

This quarter long group project was a part of the Cal Poly Human Computer Interaction course with Professor Franz Kurfess. Our team focused on the ways in which visual impairments affect the use of digital products, and researched existing laws & tools that protect and expand accessibility. We sought to design a solution that would help build empathy for users as well as assess and improve accessibility.

This project was highly collaborative as demonstrated by the final report. My individual contributions included the preliminary research report and Figma prototype listed below.
